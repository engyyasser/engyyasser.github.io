About

Simple interfaces for products with many moving parts.

My work focuses on making operational AI products easier to understand, trust, and use across support, sales, quality, knowledge, and HR workflows.

I translate complex requirements into practical product experiences, from early research and information architecture to polished UI and reusable design systems.

I also create brand identities and product visual language so each AiMY product feels distinct while still belonging to one ecosystem.
